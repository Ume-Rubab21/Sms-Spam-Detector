Text Preprocessing Pipeline
Steps Applied to Every Message
01
Lowercase Conversion
"FREE PRIZE!!" → "free prize!!" — removes case-sensitivity bias in the vocabulary.
02
Tokenisation (NLTK)
Splits text into individual tokens: "free prize!!" → ["free", "prize", "!!"]
03
Alphanumeric Filtering
Removes punctuation, symbols, and special characters. Keeps only a-z and 0-9 tokens.
04
Stopword Removal
Drops common English words like "the", "is", "and" that carry no discriminating signal for spam detection.
05
Porter Stemming
"winning" → "win", "prizes" → "prize" — maps word variants to their root, reducing vocabulary size.
06
TF-IDF Vectorisation (3,000 features)
Converts processed text to a numeric vector. TF-IDF penalises common words and rewards rare but informative ones.
